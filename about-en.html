ABOUT

Academic Research & Environmental Consulting


My name is Jennifer LAURENT, and I hold a PhD in Marine Biology and Stress Ecology from the European Institute for Marine Studies (IUEM).

My research focuses on analysing the effects of multiple pressures (pollution, climate change, hydrology) on the biodiversity of riverine and coastal ecosystems.

Throughout my career, I have developed transdisciplinary expertise spanning experimental biology, environmental data analysis, and modelling.

My work focuses on understanding the environmental and molecular signatures of anthropogenic stressors.

Beyond academic research, I have collaborated with water and environmental managers, public agencies, and local authorities to translate scientific results into decision-support tools.

Today, I offer an integrated approach combining environmental sciences, ecology, and digital technologies for the analysis and communication of complex datasets.
