DETAILED SERVICES

Services tailored to your projects

Supporting researchers, environmental consultancies, and managers in the analysis, interpretation, and valorisation of environmental data. Each service is adapted to the scientific or operational objectives of the project.

/ Data

Environmental data analysis

Transforming complex datasets into reliable and actionable results using statistical methods adapted to environmental challenges.

  • Database cleaning and structuring
  • Statistical analyses using R and Python
  • Multivariate analyses
  • Time series analyses
  • Pressure–impact relationship modelling
/ Data

Complex data visualization

Designing clear, interactive, and audience-oriented visual representations to facilitate interpretation and decision-making.

  • Simple or exploratory graphs
  • Advanced visualizations (PCA, GAM, BRT, etc.)
  • Interactive graphs for data filtering
  • Multi-scale spatial and temporal graphs
  • Dashboards and dynamic visualization
/ Digital

Digital tool development

Developing tools to efficiently explore, analyse, and share scientific data.

  • Intuitive interfaces for non-specialist users
  • Scientific platforms and dashboards
  • Web applications (Streamlit)
  • GitHub and Zenodo connectors
/ Spatial

Mapping & spatial analysis

Using geographic data to analyse environmental dynamics for different users and stakeholders.

  • Geographic data visualization and maps (QGIS)
  • Interactive maps
  • Multi-scale watershed analysis
  • Integration of GIS and environmental data
/ Decision

Science & management interface

Facilitating dialogue between researchers, managers, and stakeholders to support decision-making.

  • Communication with water managers
  • Stakeholder workshop facilitation
  • Translating results into decision-support tools
  • Development of tools adapted to user needs
/ Science

Scientific communication

Communicating scientific results to different audiences through clear, rigorous, and accessible materials.

  • Scientific article writing
  • Poster and conference preparation
  • Research result presentations
  • Various scientific communication materials
